Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

MSOON ("we", "us") values the privacy of users of "Alice Tarot Solitaire" (the "App") and complies with applicable privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

The App automatically collects the following information:

  • Advertising Identifier (Ad ID): Your device's advertising ID is collected for Google AdMob ad display.
  • Anonymous User ID: A unique identifier generated on-device (crypto.randomUUID). This cannot be used to identify individuals.
  • Device Information: Screen size, operating system version, app version
  • Gameplay Data: Stage progress, scores, completion status
  • Analytics Events: Anonymized usage statistics such as game start/end, stage clears, and ad views

Gameplay data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted externally.

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. Purpose of Collection

  • Ad delivery (rewarded and interstitial ads via Google AdMob)
  • Game progress data storage (device local)
  • Improving game service and balance
  • Bug analysis and error resolution
  • Usage statistics analysis

3. Third-Party Sharing

Collected information may be shared with the following services:

  • Google AdMob: Device identifiers and advertising IDs for ad display. See Google Privacy Policy and Google Ads Policy.
  • Google Apps Script / Sheets: Anonymized gameplay statistics storage

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Gameplay data: Stored only on your device; automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled.
  • Anonymous user ID: Automatically deleted from device when the app is uninstalled.
  • Analytics data: Retained for 1 year from collection, then deleted.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 14 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child under 14, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

6. GDPR Compliance (EEA Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we guarantee the following rights:

  • Upon first app launch, a Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) consent form will request your consent for data collection and ad personalization.
  • If you do not consent, non-personalized ads will be displayed.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time.
  • This App does not collect personally identifiable data; only anonymous UUIDs are used.

7. Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of your collected data.
  • Uninstalling the app automatically deletes all locally stored data.
  • You may opt out of personalized ads via device settings (Settings > Privacy > Advertising).
  • You may reset your advertising ID (Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID).

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will notify users through the App or this page.
