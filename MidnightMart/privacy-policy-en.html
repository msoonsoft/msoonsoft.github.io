Privacy Policy

Midnight Mart (자정의 편의점)
Effective date: May 29, 2026
Last updated: May 29, 2026
Version: v1.0

MSOON ("we", "us", "the company") operates the mobile application Midnight Mart ("the app") and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how it is used, and how it is safeguarded. We comply with the Personal Information Protection Act (Republic of Korea) and other applicable data protection laws.

🏪 In short — this app does not send your test responses to any server. All responses, results, and settings are stored exclusively on your own device, and we cannot directly access or read them.

1. Information We Process

The app does not require an account and does not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name or contact details). However, the following data is stored only on your device.

  • Test progress and choice history (guest IDs, accumulated 4-axis scores)
  • Completed test result collection (type, count, completion timestamp)
  • App settings (BGM/SFX volume, typing speed, auto-play speed, language)
  • Consent status for terms, ads, and analytics, with timestamp

This data is kept in your device's localStorage or Capacitor Preferences storage.

2. Information Collected Automatically (Ads & Analytics)

If you opt in to ads and analytics, the following may be collected automatically:

  • Advertising Identifier (AAID/IDFA) — used by Google AdMob to deliver personalized ads
  • Anonymous usage statistics — app launch/exit, test start/complete, screen transitions, etc. (individual response content is never collected)
  • Crash logs — anonymized stack traces in case of unexpected errors

This data is only collected after your explicit consent, and you can withdraw your consent at any time from the in-app settings menu.

3. Purposes of Use

  • Saving your test progress and supporting the "Resume" feature
  • Displaying your collected results
  • Preserving your personal preferences and settings
  • (With consent) showing ads and analyzing anonymous statistics to improve the service
  • (With consent) diagnosing app errors and improving stability

4. Retention Period

  • On-device data: retained until you uninstall the app or use the "Reset all data" option in settings.
  • Advertising identifier: governed by your device OS settings. Resetting your advertising ID will replace it with a new value.
  • Anonymous statistics: anonymized, aggregated data is retained by us for up to 2 years, after which it is permanently discarded.

5. Sharing and Processing by Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. The following services may operate inside the app, only with your explicit consent:

  • Google AdMob — ad delivery (Google LLC, USA). Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Apps Script (Analytics) — anonymous usage statistics (operated directly by us)

6. Security Measures

  • Local-first design: test responses are never transmitted to any server
  • Anonymous statistics are processed so that individual users cannot be identified
  • Minimum-permission principle: only Internet access is requested. Notification permission is not used in v1.0.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding all data stored by the app:

  • Access: viewable directly via the Collection screen
  • Deletion: instantly available through "Reset all data" in settings, or by uninstalling the app
  • Withdraw consent: ads and analytics consent can be withdrawn at any time from the in-app settings

8. Children's Use

The app is intended for users aged 14 and above. If users under 14 use the app, parental consent may be required. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14.

9. Disclaimer about Test Reliability

The test results provided by this app are entertainment content and are not the official MBTI® assessment from The Myers-Briggs Company. They must not be used as the basis for medical or psychological diagnosis.

11. Dispute Resolution

If you have a dispute regarding your personal information, you may contact the following bodies (Republic of Korea), or your local data protection authority if you reside outside Korea:

  • Personal Information Dispute Mediation Committee — kopico.go.kr / 1833-6972
  • Personal Information Infringement Report Center — privacy.kisa.or.kr / 118
  • Supreme Prosecutors' Office Cybercrime Investigation Unit — spo.go.kr / 1301
  • National Police Agency Cyber Bureau — ecrm.cyber.go.kr / 182

EU/EEA users may contact their local Data Protection Authority for GDPR-related inquiries.

12. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will provide notice via the in-app announcement or this page at least 7 days before the effective date. For material changes, notice will be provided at least 30 days in advance.
