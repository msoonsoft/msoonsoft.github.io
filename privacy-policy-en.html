🃏 Alice Solitaire — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

Developer: msoon

1. Information We Collect

Alice Solitaire does not directly collect any personal information.

  • No account registration required.
  • No personally identifiable information (name, email, phone) is requested.
  • No access to device data such as location, contacts, or photos.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect the following information for ad delivery:

  • Advertising Identifier (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device Information (device model, OS version)
  • IP Address
  • Ad Interaction Data (clicks, views)

This data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

If you prefer not to receive personalized ads, you can limit ad tracking in your device settings: Google Ad Settings

3. Data Storage

Game progress (stage completion, scores, settings) is stored only on the user's device (localStorage).

  • No data is transmitted to any developer server.
  • Data is deleted when the app is uninstalled.
  • No other users or third parties can access this data.

4. Network Usage

Alice Solitaire gameplay functions offline. Internet connection is used solely for loading advertisements.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will promptly delete the information.

6. User Rights (GDPR/EU)

For EU users, a consent dialog (UMP) is presented at first launch. You may decline consent, in which case non-personalized ads will be displayed.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as needed. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
