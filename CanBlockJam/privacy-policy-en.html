Privacy Policy

App: Can Block Jam  |  Last updated: April 5, 2026

msoon ("Developer") values the privacy of users of the Can Block Jam app ("App") and complies with applicable privacy laws. This policy describes what information the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • Advertising ID: Collected by Google AdMob for personalized advertising
  • Device Information: OS version, device model, etc. for ad optimization
  • Game Progress Data: Level progress, settings — stored locally on device only, not transmitted
  • App Usage Data: Ad impression and click statistics (collected anonymously by AdMob)
  • Anonymous Analytics ID: A randomly generated UUID for app usage statistics — not linked to any personal identity
  • Analytics Events: Level start/clear/fail, hint/undo usage, session duration — collected anonymously for game improvement

2. Purpose of Collection

  • Providing advertising services (banner, interstitial, rewarded ads)
  • Saving and restoring game progress
  • Improving app quality and fixing bugs

3. Third-Party Services

This App uses Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob has its own privacy policy.

📄 Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
📄 Google AdMob Partner Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Game progress data is stored only on the user's device and is automatically deleted when the App is uninstalled. Ad-related data is processed in accordance with Google's policies.

5. Children's Privacy

This App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you discover that a child's personal information has been collected, please contact us at the address below and we will promptly delete it.

6. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the App complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Consent: On first launch, users in the EEA are shown a consent form (Google UMP) to choose whether to allow personalized advertising and data collection
  • Legal Basis: Data is processed based on your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • Right of Access: You may request information about your data by contacting us
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can change your consent at any time via App Settings → "Manage Tracking"
  • Data Portability: Analytics data is anonymous and cannot be linked to an individual

If consent is denied, the App will show non-personalized ads and will not collect detailed analytics events.

7. Your Rights

  • You can manage your tracking preferences in App Settings → "Manage Tracking"
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via Device Settings → Ads → "Reset Advertising ID"
  • You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated due to changes in law or service. Changes will be announced via in-app notice or this page.
